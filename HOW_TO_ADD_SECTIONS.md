# How to Add Sections to Your Homepage

## Quick Steps:

1. **In Shopify Theme Editor:**
   - Look at the **left sidebar** where it says "Home page"
   - You should see a **grid icon** (sections icon) - click it
   - You'll see a list of available sections

2. **Add Sections:**
   - Click **"Add section"** button at the bottom
   - You'll see all available sections:
     - Hero Section
     - Product Collection
     - Trust Badges
     - Newsletter
     - Artist Feature
     - CTA Section

3. **Add Each Section:**
   - Click "Add section" for each one
   - They will appear in order on your homepage
   - Drag and drop to reorder them

4. **Customize:**
   - Click any section in the left sidebar
   - Use the right panel to customize colors, text, images, etc.

## Expected Order:
1. Hero Section (full-screen hero)
2. Product Collection (product grid)
3. Trust Badges (4 trust indicators)
4. Newsletter (two-column signup)
5. Artist Feature (two-column showcase)
6. CTA Section (call-to-action)

All sections are fully customizable with extensive options!
